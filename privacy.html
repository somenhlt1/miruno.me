Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 13, 2024

Introduction

Miruno ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using Miruno, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

Information We Collect

Location Data

Miruno collects precise GPS location data to track your mileage and routes. This includes:

  • Real-time GPS coordinates during active tracking sessions
  • Start and end locations of your trips
  • Route paths and distances traveled
  • Timestamps associated with location data

Background Location: With your permission, Miruno may collect location data even when the app is closed or not in use to enable automatic trip detection.

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Profile preferences

Vehicle Information

Information you provide about your vehicles, including:

  • Vehicle make, model, and year
  • Vehicle nicknames
  • Odometer readings (if provided)

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Services: Track mileage, log trips, and generate reports for tax deduction purposes
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Communicate: Send important updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Meet legal requirements and respond to lawful requests

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption methods. We use Supabase for cloud storage, which provides:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Regular security audits and compliance certifications

Trip data is also stored locally on your device to ensure functionality even without internet connectivity.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Third-Party Services We Use

  • Google Maps: For map display and route visualization
  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • Sentry: For crash reporting and error tracking

Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your trip data in standard formats (CSV, PDF)
  • Withdraw Consent: Disable location permissions at any time through your device settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@miruno.app

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Trip logs are retained to help you maintain records for tax purposes. You may delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual trips within the app
  • Deleting your entire account through Settings
  • Contacting us to request complete data deletion

Children's Privacy

Miruno is not intended for use by children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes
